Passion Fruit Pudding

History

Here is a step by step how to make quick passion fruit pudding in a blender that does not go in the oven and does not go on fire. Passionfruit Pudding with simple blender syrup that is a delicious! Make it for any special date or for everyday life.

Ingredients

Pudding Filling

  1. 500 ml milk
  2. 3 envelopes of passion fruit gelatin
  3. 1 carton of cream
  4. 1 carton of condensed milk

  1. pulp of 2 passion fruits
  2. 1 cup (tea) of sugar
  3. 1 cup (tea) of water

Pudding Directions

  1. In a blender, add the milk, the gelatine, the heavy cream, and the condensed milk.
  2. Whisk it all together for about 2 minutes or until smooth.
  3. Pour this mixture into a lightly greased mold.
  4. NOTE: The oil will help when unmolding, but it does not taste like oil.
  5. Refrigerate for 3 hours or until firm.

Coating Ingredients

  1. In a pan, add the pulp, sugar, and water.
  2. Bring to medium heat and stir until it boils and thickens a little.
  3. Remove the pudding from the refrigerator, unmold it, and pour the syrup over it.
